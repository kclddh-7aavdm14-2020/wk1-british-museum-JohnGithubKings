Google Arts & Culture

It is a very well made website. It is very versatile and fast and its contents are of very high quality. It is playful and apparently chaotic but at the same time you can find what you are looking for very easily. You can obviously consult an exuberant amount of artworks that can be viewed with the most modern display systems.

Interactivity

One of the biggest potential of this site is undoubtedly its strong interactive component. in fact you can participate in quick surveys, play mini games and much more with impressive ease. The speed of response of the site is amazing especially when you consider the high level of detail it offers.
