Variety

It is a website used for the storage and preservation of the most varied and disparate contents. Inside it are freely available and downloadable many contents such as: books, software, movies and much more in a simple and free way. This site supports the idea of free information and liquid culture. Personally I am very much in favor of this kind of initiatives and I believe that, if you respect a period of a few months from the release of the work, it is a correct way to circulate culture. Obviously this may bother the owners of the rights of the works in question, but I do not want to go into this discussion.

Usability

One of the features that immediately jumped out at me of this site is the fact that there are no ads or advertisements of any kind and that the layout is very simple and modular, designed to optimize the search and speed of loading pages. Probably it is preloaded html and css code and not a server that generates the pages every time. I could be wrong about this but probably so.
