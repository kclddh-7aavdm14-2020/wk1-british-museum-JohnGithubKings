Bio

Hello, My name is John Bianchi and I come from the University of Pisa. I currently live in Lucca which is also my hometown. I have a Bachelor's degree in Digital Humanities in Pisa and I have worked for several projects such as data scraping, front-end developing and technical assistance. I liked this website of the Italian museum M9 even if I don't agree with the idea of using a video that starts automatically when you log in. https://www.m9museum.it/ It is a pleasure to participate in this course and I can't wait to get started.
