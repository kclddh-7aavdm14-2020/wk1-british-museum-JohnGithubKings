McCord Museum, Canada

For this website I have to make a generally negative comment. I was, in fact, disappointed by many aspects of the site.

Structure of the site

the site has in itself a poorly maintained structure that seems to be the result of some CMS like Wordpress used even in a hurry. There are in fact space holes already in the homepage. Moreover, much stranger, is the fact that the "archive" section of this web presents a totally different style from the rest of the site. In addition, always in this section the style is much more dated as if it were a part of the site that has not been updated.

Contents

The works that the museum exhibits are available online only in a low-resolution form and without any possibility of interaction. In addition, the catalog is very limited and the search for objects is poorly managed.
